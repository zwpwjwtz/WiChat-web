X				1byte Account State
X(x48)				Max 48bytes Email Address
X(x32)				Max 32bytes Phone Number
X(x32)				Max 32bytes Authentication Question
X(x32)				Max 32bytes Authentication Response
X(x44)				42bytes Reserved
